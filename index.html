Planting trees

Life is like a paddock, and we are the farmers. In the beginning the paddock is just an empty field. As time goes on the farmer is given seeds, and they decide which seeds to grow. As the farmer plants the trees, he must nurture them, supplying them with nutrients for them to grow. Growing a tree is a long and tiresome process, but with luck, perserverance and time a seed can grow into a tree. But the job is not done then, as the tree must continually be watered. The trees become the pillars of achievement which prop up one's sense of self. They are the anchors which make up an individual.
